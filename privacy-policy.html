Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how our application collects, uses, and protects user information.

Information We Collect

Camera Permission

The app uses the device camera only for capturing photos or scanning features inside the app. Images captured by the camera are not shared with third parties and are used only for app functionality.

Location Permission

The app may access the device location to provide location-based features such as maps or nearby services. Location data is used only while using the app and is not stored or shared.

Bluetooth Permission

The app uses Bluetooth to connect with nearby printers in order to print order receipts or related documents. Bluetooth access is used only for device connectivity and no personal data is collected through Bluetooth.

How We Use Information

The permissions requested are used only to provide the core functionality of the application.

  • Camera – capture images inside the app
  • Location – show maps and location-based features
  • Bluetooth – connect to printers for printing receipts

Third Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as Google Maps that may collect information according to their privacy policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data and ensure it is not misused.
